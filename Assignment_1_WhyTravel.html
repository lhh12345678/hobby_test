Why Travel

There are many reasons to travel.



Each person has their own reasons for travel and I have already talked about mine (in the About Me section) but in a grander sense how can one look at the world and not be transported by wonder, embracing the desire to see these stunning places with your own eyes. We evolved to live on this beautiful planet, unique in our solar system. We only get to explore for about 80 or 90 years, then we leave it forever, go explore!!



People are curious by nature, why not indulge that inner child and see the wonders of the world!
