Why Travel


Future Travel

To quote the Lion Ling "There is more to see than can ever be seen and more to do than can ever be done"


The world is an amazing place with so many locations that I hope to explore. It helps to have a plan or even a general idea of where one wants to go but there is also the future of travel itself to think about!


My hopes for future travel destinations includes New Zealsnd, China via the ancient silk road, Madagascar, Egypt, Botswana, Zimbabwe, Ethiopia, Brazil, Mexico, Argentina, Japan, Morocco. Really I could go in and on listing my dream destinations but due to the impact on our planet travel is uncertain. How we are getting where we want to go without doing more damage?.


I greatly enjoy traveling by train and bus as I don't have to stress about being the driver. Flying is also an amazing experience that never gets old but there are new inovation in air travel that I am watching closely. Reintroducing airtravel by Zeplin! One worry that I often hold while traveling is the damage caused to the environment by how I travel so I look into alternative ways and green options to get to where I want to go.
