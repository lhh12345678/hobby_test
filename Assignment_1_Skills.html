Why Travel


Skills

During traveling there are many skills that you pick up because you need to and many that you can get because you want to.


Its never to late to learn something new. From time management and bag organisation to diving and horse back riding, all very useful. More practical skills are in language and non-verbal communication. Learning to make a meal out of whatever you have with you is handy and caring for small wounds with basic first aid is vital knowledge. There is a wide range of skill that can be learned while traveling.



The video to the right will give a brief listing of all kinds of fun skills that can be learned!


From the little video's list I can definately say that my top 3 skills to learn are Horse back riding, Archery and Diving.
